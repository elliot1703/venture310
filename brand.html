Usage Rules

Type Rules

  • Headlines: uppercase with letter-spacing 0.15em–0.25em for brand headers

Wordmark Rules

  • Cormorant Garamond, weight 600, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.2em

[007] Voice & Tone

Direct. Assured. Never salesy.

✓ Use

  • "Your story is your greatest competitive advantage"
  • "Attention, awareness, profitability"
  • "Storytelling" instead of "content strategy"
  • "Attention" instead of "impressions"
  • Short, punchy sentences mixed with narrative flow
  • Speaking to business owners, not at them

✗ Never

  • "Trusted partner"
  • "Committed to excellence"
  • "Your journey starts here"
  • "We understand that..."
  • Hype-driven language or fake urgency
  • Marketing jargon (KPIs, synergy, leverage)

Sentence Rhythm Example

The businesses that win aren't the biggest. They're the ones that tell their story better than anyone else. We're the team that makes that happen.

[008] Motion & Animation

Confident and unhurried.

Ease-out curves, 300–500ms durations. Motion should feel assured, never frantic.

Fade Up (Scroll Reveal)

Your Story

Hover Scale (Buttons)

Hover Me

Scale 1.02, 200ms ease, subtle shadow increase

Motion Rules

  • Entrance: fade-up, staggered for groups (80–100ms between items)
  • Hover: gentle scale (1.02) or colour shift, never jarring
  • Durations: 300–500ms for reveals, 200ms for hover states
  • Easing: ease-out for entrances, ease for hover
  • Respect prefers-reduced-motion — disable all animations
[009] Anti-Slop Checklist

Quality gate. Every asset. No exceptions.

Visual

  • Green + Cream used for primary brand moments
  • No green text on cream at small sizes
  • Accents at 5–10% of composition max
  • Cormorant for display, DM Sans for body
  • Headlines tracked and uppercase where appropriate
  • Generous whitespace — nothing cramped
  • Border radius 8–12px on cards
  • Images warm, aspirational, Dubai-contextual

Brand & Voice

  • No banned phrases ("trusted partner", "excellence")
  • Speaks to owners, not at prospects
  • "Storytelling" not "content strategy"
  • Copy couldn't be swapped to another agency
  • Value proposition visible within 3 seconds
  • CTA is clear and proportional to commitment
  • Animations ease-out, 300–500ms
  • Reduced-motion users see no animation
